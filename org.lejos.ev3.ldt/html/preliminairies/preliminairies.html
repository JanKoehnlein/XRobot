Preliminaries

To use the Eclipse plugin for leJOS EV3, you need to have the following requirements fulfilled:

  1. A distribution of leJOS EV3 installed on your local machine
  2. The right path as EV3_HOME is set in the the leJOS Ev3 section of the Preferences page of Eclipse

For requirements of leJOS EV3 itself, consult the leJOS EV3 Wiki.
